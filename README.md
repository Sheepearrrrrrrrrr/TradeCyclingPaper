## 📃 Trade Cycling Paper
Use the [Trade Cycling mod by Max Henkel](https://modrinth.com/mod/trade-cycling) on PaperMC servers.

[![ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/img/githubbutton_sm.svg)](https://ko-fi.com/H2H118OP6T)

---

## Requirements

* **Server:** Paper 1.21.11+ (or forks like Purpur)
* **Client:** Players who want to cycle trades **must** have the [Trade Cycling Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/trade-cycling) installed in their Minecraft client.

> **Note:** Players without the mod can still join the server normally, but they will not be able to use the trade cycling keybind or widget.

---

## Installation & Usage

1. Download the `.jar` for your version from the [Releases](https://github.com/your-repo/releases) page.
2. Drop it into your server's `plugins/` folder and restart the server.
3. Open a villager trading GUI and press the cycle trades keybind (configured in the client mod) or click the widget.

For full details on client controls, keybinds, and mod settings, visit the [original Modrinth page](https://modrinth.com/mod/trade-cycling).
